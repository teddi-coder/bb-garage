Common Questions

Questions We Get Asked About BB Garage

Why does my 4WD keep losing power? +
Often clogged fuel filters, turbo issues, or dirty sensors. Our diesel diagnostics team will pinpoint the problem fast and explain exactly what’s happening before touching anything.
How long will repairs take? +
Many jobs are completed same-day. For bigger repairs, we offer loan cars and flexible scheduling so you’re never left stuck.
Why are other mechanics giving me different answers? +
Big shops often rush assessments. At BB Garage, we take the time to properly inspect and explain — so you can make an informed decision without any pressure.
Can I still go offroad with performance upgrades? +
Absolutely — we specialise in setups that enhance offroad ability, not compromise it. Everything we install is tested and tuned for real-world conditions.
Can I do this myself? +
YouTube won’t help with high-pressure fuel systems or electronic diagnostics. Leave the tricky stuff to the pros — it’s cheaper in the long run.
