Engine Health

Why You Need a Catch Can & Intake Clean

Oil vapours from your crankcase system coat your intake, reduce airflow, and slowly kill performance. We treat these as non-negotiables.

  • Intake Manifold Cleaning — removes carbon build-up that chokes airflow and power
  • Catch Can Install — traps oil vapours before they re-enter the intake system

One of the best investments you can make for long-term engine health — especially if you’re tuning for more power.
