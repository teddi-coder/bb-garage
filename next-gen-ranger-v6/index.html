BB Garage is different. Every V6 tune we do is:

Common Questions

Questions About V6 Diesel Tuning

Is tuning safe on these new V6 models? +
Yes — when done correctly. That’s exactly why we use mechanical inspections before the tune, in-house dyno testing throughout, and post-tune verification. We don’t upload files and hope for the best.
Why does my V6 keep losing power? +
Often clogged fuel filters, turbo issues, or dirty sensors — but on V6 platforms it can also be factory torque limiting strategies cutting in. Our diagnostics team will pinpoint the cause fast and explain it clearly before touching anything.
Why are other mechanics giving me different answers? +
Big shops often rush assessments and many simply don’t have the tooling for these platforms. At BB Garage, we take the time to properly inspect and explain — so you can make an informed decision without pressure.
Can I still go offroad after the tune? +
Absolutely — we specialise in setups that enhance offroad ability, not compromise it. Everything is tuned and dyno-verified for real-world Australian conditions, not just a highway dyno run.
Can I do this myself? +
YouTube won’t help with encrypted ECUs or high-pressure fuel systems. The V6 platforms specifically require specialist access tools that most workshops — let alone DIYers — don’t have. Leave it to the pros.
